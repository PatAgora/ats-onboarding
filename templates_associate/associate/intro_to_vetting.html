    An Introduction to Vetting

    This guide will help you understand the key checks we conduct and why they are important. Note that additional checks may be required based on client needs or your unique circumstances.

    A dedicated Compliance Analyst will support you throughout the entire process to ensure a smooth and timely start.

    Please click on the headings below to find out more information. If you would like to know more about where your data will be stored, please see our privacy policy.

    Why is this required?

    These documents confirm an individual's identity and reduce the risk of employing someone who may be impersonating another person.

    What documents are acceptable?
    Proof of Identity:
    • In-date Signed Passport
    • In-date UK Full Photo Card Driver's Licence
    Proof of Address:
    • Bank/Building Society/Credit Card statement (dated within 90 days of start date)
    • Utility Bill (dated within 90 days of start date)
    • Council Tax Bill / Mortgage Statement (dated within 12 months of start date)
    • In-date UK Full Driver's Licence (if not being used as ID)

    This is not an exhaustive list. Please contact your assigned Compliance Analyst to discuss alternative options.

    Why are they required?

    These forms provide us with your consent to conduct the necessary background checks and offer you the opportunity to disclose any information that might affect the vetting process. You may also be required to complete client-specific forms.

    How do I complete these documents?

    You can complete the required forms directly through this portal:

    • Consent Form — consent to vetting checks
    • Declaration Form — fit and proper declaration

    On occasion, additional documents may need to be hand-signed. Your Compliance Analyst will be in touch if this is required.

    Why is this required?

    Our clients require us to carry out a basic criminal record and financial probity check to help understand our associates' history and assess potential risks.

    How is this carried out?

    We use a third-party supplier to carry out these checks. In most instances, we are required to verify identity and address documents via a video call beforehand. After the call, you will receive a link to an application form by email.

    It's important this application is completed as soon as possible as the client typically requires these checks to be complete prior to your start date. On average, these checks take around five working days, but can take longer.

    Why is this required?

    Reference checks help verify the information that you have provided on your CV and throughout your application, as well as verify your activity during a specified period. Most of our clients require three years' referencing. This includes all employment that you have undertaken during this period, as well as education and periods of unemployment.

    As reference checks are dependent on a third party, we will ask you to download your HMRC Employment History at the start of the process to use as alternative evidence whilst we wait for responses.

    How to download your HMRC Employment History: Visit HMRC online, sign in, select ‘Pay As You Earn (PAYE)’ and navigate to ‘View and print Income Tax and employment history.’ Then select ‘Print this page’.
    Additional Evidence

    If additional evidence is required to cover missing references or periods of non-employment, here are some examples of what we may accept (your Compliance Analyst will confirm what is required for your specific circumstances):

    Missing references:
    • Payslips / Bank Statements
    • P60 / P45
    • Employment Contract
    • Personal Work Reference
    • Professional Relationship Reference
    Periods of non-employment:
    • Seeking Employment: JSA/Universal Credit Statements, Bank Statements
    • Travelling: Passport Stamps, Flight/Hotel Confirmation
    • Maternity/Paternity: Child's birth certificate

    What are they?

    An Umbrella/Employment company operates as an employer for contractors working on a fixed term assignment through Optimus Solutions. They offer a straightforward and stress-free route into work, which is particularly helpful if you are new to contracting.

    When should I register?

    Though registration is simple, the umbrella company may need to carry out their own checks, which can take a few days. It is therefore important that you register as soon as possible, as we are unable to issue your contract without a completed registration.

    If you register and do not end up commencing on the project, you will not be charged.

    How long will my vetting take to complete?

    This will vary from person to person as we gather information from external sources. However, at the start of the vetting process, your Compliance Analyst will confirm the deadline for your assigned intake.

    What happens if my file isn't completed in time?

    Depending on what is missing, your Compliance Analyst may ask you to provide alternative evidence in the interim. We will use this evidence to refer your file to the client for approval. This helps us to onboard you for your intended start date.

    What if I refuse to complete vetting?

    Our clients require all associates to successfully complete the vetting process to a satisfactory level. Refusing to comply will result in your job offer being withdrawn.

    What can I do to speed up the vetting process?

    Provide the requested information in a timely manner and help chase external sources where possible.

    Do I need to send Optimus Solutions a paper copy of my DBS check?

    No. We receive confirmation from the provider as to whether your DBS is clear or not. If your DBS has convictions listed however, we will need you to send a picture of your DBS certificate to us once received in the post.

    When will I receive my contract & IT equipment?

    With confirmation from our client, and your vetting file being successfully completed, you will typically receive your contract and IT equipment within five days of your intended start date. You will receive confirmation of this closer to your start date.
